About Us

We are pioneering the future of healthcare documentation in India by transforming doctor-patient consultations into accurate, real-time records through advanced AI technology. Our solution seamlessly enhances teleconsultation workflows, enabling healthcare professionals to focus entirely on patient care.

By automating the documentation process, we eliminate the need for manual note-taking, reducing errors and saving valuable time. Our technology supports a wide range of Indian languages, ensuring healthcare professionals across the country can provide high-quality care that is both accessible and inclusive.

By streamlining documentation, our solution improves operational efficiency, enhances patient care, and helps healthcare providers manage their patients more effectively. With AI handling the details of note-taking, doctors can connect more meaningfully with patients, resulting in a smoother and more efficient telemedicine experience for all.
